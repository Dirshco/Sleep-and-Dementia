Comprehensive PRISMA Flowcharts and Search Strategies

Below are structured PRISMA flowcharts and search strategy syntax for 10 systematic review topics exploring the relationship between sleep-wake disorders and Alzheimer's disease. Each includes detailed search terms, PRISMA flowchart templates, and key filters for database searches.

1. Impact of Sleep Disruption on Alzheimer's Risk

Search Strategy (PubMed):

("Alzheimer Disease"[Mesh] OR "Dementia"[Mesh] OR "Alzheimer*"[tiab] OR "AD"[tiab]) AND ("Sleep Deprivation"[Mesh] OR "Sleep Wake Disorders"[Mesh] OR "insomnia"[tiab] OR "poor sleep"[tiab] OR "short sleep"[tiab]) AND ("Risk Factors"[Mesh] OR "longitudinal"[tiab] OR "cohort"[tiab] OR "prospective"[tiab])

PRISMA Flowchart:

  1. Records identified: X (PubMed) + Y (Embase) + Z (other) = Total (N)
  2. After duplicates removed: N1
  3. Screened titles/abstracts: N2 excluded, N3 eligible
  4. Full-text assessed: N4 excluded (reasons), N5 included
Key Filters: Longitudinal studies, human subjects, English language, publication years 2000-2024

2. Sleep-Disordered Breathing (SDB) and AD Pathology

Search Strategy (Embase):

('Alzheimer disease'/exp OR 'dementia'/exp OR 'alzheimer*':ti,ab) AND ('sleep disordered breathing'/exp OR 'obstructive sleep apnea'/exp OR 'osa':ti,ab OR 'sdb':ti,ab) AND ('amyloid beta'/exp OR 'tau protein'/exp OR 'biomarker*':ti,ab OR 'neuroimaging':ti,ab)

PRISMA Flowchart:

  1. Records identified: X (Embase) + Y (PubMed) + Z (other) = Total (N)
  2. After duplicates removed: N1
  3. Screened titles/abstracts: N2 excluded, N3 eligible
  4. Full-text assessed: N4 excluded (reasons), N5 included
Key Filters: Cohort, case-control, cross-sectional studies; years 2000-2024

Key Tips for Your Systematic Review
