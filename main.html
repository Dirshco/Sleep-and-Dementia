    Frequently Asked Questions

    How many databases should I search?

    At minimum, search PubMed/MEDLINE, Embase, and Cochrane Central. Consider additional subject-specific databases like PsycINFO or CINAHL depending on your topic.

    How do I handle non-English studies?

    Include non-English studies if you have translation capabilities, or state language restrictions in your methods. Document how many non-English studies were excluded during screening.

    What's the best way to document my search process?

    Keep detailed records of:

    • Search dates and database versions
    • Complete search strategies for each database
    • Number of results from each source
    • Screening decisions at each phase
